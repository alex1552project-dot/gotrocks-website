Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering and delivery

For orders placed before noon within our Zone 1 delivery area (0-10 miles from Conroe), we can often deliver same-day. Most orders are delivered within 24-48 hours. You'll see available delivery dates when you complete your quote.

Use our Material Calculator! Enter your project dimensions (length × width × depth) and we'll calculate the cubic yards needed. As a general rule: 1 cubic yard covers about 100 square feet at 3 inches deep.

We always recommend ordering slightly more than you think you need (about 10% extra) to account for settling and spreading. If you end up needing more, we can usually do a follow-up delivery within a day or two.

Our drivers will dump materials on your driveway, street (with your permission), or designated area that's accessible by truck. Please ensure the drop area is clear and accessible. Our trucks need about 10 feet of clearance width.

Simple: we're the actual supplier, not a broker. Other sites take your order, then find a local supplier like us to fulfill it—and they add their markup. When you order from Texas Got Rocks, you're going direct. Same materials, same trucks, but without the middleman fees. That's how we can offer better prices AND better service—plus always FREE delivery!

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) through our secure online checkout. Payment is processed when you place your order, and your price is locked in—no surprise charges at delivery.
